timeline

Our Journey So Far

From an idea to empower growth through education and coaching, here’s how Pathwise has evolved—one milestone at a time
2017
The Idea Takes Shape
Inspired by the need for accessible coaching and structured learning, Pathwise was envisioned as a platform to guide individuals and teams toward personal and professional growth
2018
First Programs Launched
We introduced our Foundations Program and Leadership workshops, creating structured pathways for skill-building and confidence development.
2020
Expanding Our Reach
Pathwise grew beyond in-person sessions, offering online seminars and hybrid learning options to reach students, professionals, and teams worldwide.
2022
Community & Success Stories
With a growing alumni network, Pathwise highlighted client successes through case studies and testimonials, strengthening our community impact.
2024
Scaling with Innovation
We launched new advanced strategy tracks and integrated digital tools, making it easier than ever for clients and schools to access coaching, track progress, and achieve measurable outcomes.
